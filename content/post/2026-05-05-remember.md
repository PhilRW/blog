Hey. People. Here are some *basic* things you need to have memorized:

- Your birthday
- Your anniversary
- Your Social Security Number
- Your phone number
- At least one other person's phone number
- Your home address
- Your car's license plate number
- Your immediate family members' birthdays
- The password to your password manager

If you can't remember these basic things, **try harder**.
