title: Automatic Lemon
wordpress_id: 166

lemon automatic
dishwasher detergent

automatic transmission
stop and go

automatic receptionist
press 1 for cold machine
2 for human interaction
but not before more cold machine

automatic camera
point and shoot
click and capture
no more memories

automatic life
processed humanity
shrink wrapped for your protection

automatic love
wash rinse repeat
until death
